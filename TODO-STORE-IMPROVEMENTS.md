# Store & Seller Features - Future Improvements

This document tracks planned improvements for the seller storefront and store management features.

## Store Customization

- [ ] **Edit Store button** - Add an "Edit Store" or "Customize" button on the store page when viewing your own store
- [ ] **Store bio** - Allow a separate marketplace-specific bio (currently uses Bluesky bio)
- [ ] **Contact info** - Option to display preferred contact method or business hours
- [ ] **Store branding** - Custom store name, tagline, or theme colors

## Store Management

- [ ] **Quick actions from store page** - "Add New Listing" button visible on your own store
- [ ] **Pin/feature listings** - Allow sellers to pin certain listings to the top of their store
- [ ] **Store categories/sections** - Let sellers organize their listings into custom sections
- [ ] **Bulk listing management** - Select multiple listings to edit/delete/archive

## Analytics & Insights

- [ ] **Store analytics** - Show view counts, click-through rates on listings
- [ ] **Listing performance** - Track which listings get the most interest
- [ ] **Visitor insights** - See how many people viewed your store

## Discovery & SEO

- [ ] **Store search** - Allow browsing/searching stores (not just listings)
- [ ] **Featured sellers** - Highlight top sellers or new stores
- [ ] **Store ratings/reviews** - Allow buyers to rate their experience

## External Commerce Integration

- [ ] **More affiliate platforms** - Add support for additional platforms
- [ ] **Custom affiliate IDs** - Let sellers use their own affiliate IDs
- [ ] **Link analytics** - Track clicks on external buy links
